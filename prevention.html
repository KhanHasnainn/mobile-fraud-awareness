How to Stay Safe:-

  • Never share OTP with anyone
  • Do not click unknown links
  • Use strong passwords
  • Enable two factor authentication
  • Download apps only from official stores
  • Keep phone software updated
  • Verify calls claiming to be from banks or customer care.
  • Do not approve unknown UPI payment requests or scan random QR codes.
  • Stay alert and aware about new types of online and mobile frauds.
