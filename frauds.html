Common Frauds

OTP Scam:-

An OTP scam is a type of online fraud where scammers try to trick people into sharing their One Time Password (OTP). OTP is a security code sent to a user's mobile number during online transactions, banking activities, or account logins.

UPI Frauds:-

UPI fraud is a type of online scam where fraudsters trick people into sending money through UPI apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm. They may send fake payment requests, ask users to scan QR codes, or pretend to be sellers or bank officials.

Phishing Links:-

Phishing links are fake links sent through SMS, email, or social media that try to trick people into entering their personal information such as bank details, passwords, or OTP

Fake Apps:-

Fake apps are fraudulent mobile applications that look similar to real apps. When people download and use these apps, scammers can steal personal data, banking information, or gain access to the phone.
